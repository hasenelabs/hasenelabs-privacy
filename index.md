# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

HaseneLabs ("we", "our", or "us") operates mobile applications that provide religious and informational content, including prayer times, reminders, and related features.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
We may collect approximate location information to determine prayer times based on the user's city or region. Location data is used solely for this purpose and is not stored or shared.

### Notifications
We request permission to send notifications to remind users of prayer times. Notifications are optional and can be disabled at any time from the device settings.

### Personal Data
We do not collect, store, or share personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.

## How We Use Information
- To calculate prayer times based on location
- To send prayer time reminders (if enabled by the user)
- To improve app functionality and user experience

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or share user data with third parties.

## Data Security
We take reasonable measures to protect user information. All data processing occurs locally on the device unless explicitly stated.

## Children's Privacy
Our applications do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
